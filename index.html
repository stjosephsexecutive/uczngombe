OUR MISSION & VISION

Holistic Transformation in Christ

St. Joseph's Congregation, under the United Church of Zambia, exists to spread the Good News of salvation, engaging in holistic community development and service in fulfillment of Christ's mission to the world.

✝️ Total Salvation

Spiritual restoration and physical well-being, fully reconciled in Christ, the only Saviour.

🌍 Reconciliation & Empowerment

Training members for evangelism, discipleship, and community service — living out Christ's mission.
